Why do people play on mobile?

Mobile has always been a platform for gaming since games were a thing. It didn't used to have many complex games as we do now such as clash of clans, subway surfer, or candy crush. Snake used to be a game on mobile devices that was around in the 1990’s and it was popular. It was probably one of the first games to be on a mobile platform. Mobile games started to evolve from this and now we see the games we do now. Mobile games aren’t as competitive as the other platforms and are usually played casually. Strategic games are probably one of the popular genres of mobile games because they are usually a little complicated which make them more fun to play.
